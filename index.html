English Level One
Designed in 2017 to help students to use this open-source learning platform, so it can be impletmented in Japanese schools. Both teachers and students can easily access to these digital worksheets and other interactive activities. They can be used in the classroom or at home. Here are some examples:

  • Lesson 1-01: I am ~. You are ~.
  • Lesson 1-02: Are you ~. Are you from ~.
  • Lesson 1-03: This is my ~. That is your ~.
  • Lesson 1-04: Is this ~. Is that ~.
  • Lesson 1-05: He/She is ~.

1. New Horizon 2. New Crown 3. Sunshine
